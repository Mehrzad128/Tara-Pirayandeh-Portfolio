BIOGRAPHY

Tara Pirayandeh was born on September 21, 2002, in Isfahan, Iran.

Tara Pirayandeh has been immersed in art, music, and literature from an early age. At just 5, she began her musical journey and quickly mastered the Tombak. Influenced by legendary artists like Jalil Shahnaz , Mohammadreza Lotfi , Hooshang Kamkar and Pashang Kamkar , she transitioned to the Tar in 2011.

Her studies in this traditional Persian instrument have been guided by renowned masters including Hossein Alizadeh . Tara's extensive training also includes workshops with celebrated musicians such as Bahman Rajabi , Shahram Miljalali , Ali Jahandar , Ali Asghar Shahzaidi and Amir Eslami. She has performed at numerous festivals and concerts across Iran and internationally, showcasing her exceptional artistry and deep connection to Persian music.

A prodigy with an extraordinary intellect, Tara accelerated her education, graduating from high school at the age of 13. As a member of Iran's National Elites Foundation, she has actively participated in charitable endeavors, including supporting children with cancer and donating all her awards to this cause. Her dedication extends to performing charity concerts across various Iranian cities, using her music as a means of compassion and healing.

At 17, Tara earned her bachelor's degree in Computer Science and later received a scholarship to attend Berklee College of Music in Boston, Massachusetts. There, she is pursuing a major in Film Scoring with a minor in Conducting. Her notable performances include a Nowruz celebration for VOA Channel in Washington, D.C., and performing as a soloist with the Iranshahr orchestra at Royce Hall , UCLA (Los Angeles), and Herbst Theater Hall (San Francisco).

Currently, Tara's primary artistic focus lies in film scoring , where she channels her deep understanding of traditional and modern music into creating emotionally powerful soundtracks. She is especially passionate about composing for animation, film, and documentary , blending Persian musical elements with contemporary cinematic language to tell stories that transcend borders. After years of studying diverse musical traditions, Tara now devotes herself to crafting soundscapes that evoke feeling, narrative, and cultural connection on screen.

Tara Pirayandeh is devoted to expanding the global reach of Persian and traditional music. Through her innovative work and her growing body of compositions for visual media, she strives to share the emotional depth and cultural richness of her heritage with audiences around the world.
