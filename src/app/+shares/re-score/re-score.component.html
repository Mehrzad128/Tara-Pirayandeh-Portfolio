Spring
A poetic animated short film produced by Blender Studio. The film follows a young shepherdess and her dog as they face ancient spirits to bring renewal to their valley, symbolizing the eternal cycle of life, death, and rebirth. The score intertwines lush orchestral colors with ethereal choral textures, evoking both the grandeur of nature and the emotional depth of the protagonist's journey, Through its dynamic interplay of melody and atmosphere. Score and orchestration by Tara Pirayandeh.
Sprite Fright
An animated short film produced by Blender Studio. The film offers a playful reinterpretation of classic horror motifs, centering on a group of teenagers who encounter mischievous forest sprites with unexpectedly dark intentions. The score combines orchestral and atmospheric elements to accentuate the film's blend of humor, tension, and fantasy. Composition and orchestration by Tara Pirayandeh.
